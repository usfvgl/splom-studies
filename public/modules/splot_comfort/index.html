Scatterplots

Pictured below is an example scatterplot visualization for variable z encoded by horizontal position and variable v encoded by vertical position. Each circle is colored by its class, or category.

For example, the circles in purple have high values of z and v and belong to the same class. The red circles have z values near 5 and v values under 5. The blue, teal, and gold circles have overlapping values for both variables.
